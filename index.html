About Ashish

Ashish is a script writer based in India, developing stories that lean into emotion and momentum. His work spans features, pilots, and branded pieces, with collaborations across production houses and independent studios.

He believes every scene should do two things: move the plot and move the audience. If it doesn’t, it gets cut.
